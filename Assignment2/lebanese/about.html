Shisha

Lebanese water pipe culture

Shisha is a social pastime enjoyed in the Middle East. It is known by many names, including shisha, Mu‘assel, hookah, hubble-bubble and water-pipe. Shisha includes the use of flavoured tobacco-mix and syrups which is smoked through a hose.

Belly Dancing

Belly dancing is an artform found in the Middle East, from Cairo (Egypt) to Beirut (Lebanon). It is an art form featured throughout the years from films to popular artist performances. Seen here is Shakira who is known for incorporating belly dancing into her routines.
