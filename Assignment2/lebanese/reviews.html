Mejana Restaurant LIMERICK ✭✭✭✭

Mejana Restaurant is located on bustling Thomas Street in the heart of Limerick City. Enjoy a dazzling array of delectable Lebanese cuisine while enjoying the art of Lebanese Belly Dancing or relax with friends over the Shisha water pipe. The Apple Mint shisha is public favourite.
Read more about Mejana Limerick on 'TripAdvisor'.

Cedar House LIMERICK ✭✭✭✭

The award-winning Cedar house recently moved into Robert Street beside the iconic Haymarket and Milkmarket of Limerick. A sister restaurant to Mejana, Cedar House serves a fresh new menu catering for the tastebuds of casual diners and wine buffs alike.
